10 Great British bikini babes The Sun

IT’S bikini week in The Sun — and we've rounded up the very best of the British bikini babes.

From Kelly Brook to our very own Lorraine Kelly, from Abbey Clancy to Dame Helen Mirren, we’ve picked ten of our favourite shore shots to spur you on.

Be-cossie you’re worth it.

Michelle Keegan

YOU can see why the former Coronation Street bombshell, 28, was voted the world’s sexiest woman by FHM.

Kelly Brook

BRITAIN’S bikini queen, 35, has just lost 8lbs and is a curvy size ten. That’ll be a perfect ten, then.

Kimberley Garner

NO wonder the Made in Chelsea babe, 25, has her own bikini range.

Elizabeth Hurley

GUESS what? The actress, 49, has her own beachwear line — and has had for a decade.

She said: “I don’t think growing older means looking less good.” Judging by this pic, we’d agree that being in the swim suits her.

Helen Mirren

PROVING there really is nothing like a Dame, the actress peeled off on a family holiday in Italy.

Helen, 69, said modestly of the stunning snap: “I think it will haunt me for the rest of my life.”

Abbey Clancy

THE model and fitness fan, 29, has just had her second child — and will always have a ma-vellous following.

Alex Gerrard

STEVIE’S wife, 32, has tanning chances galore now he’s going to LA.

Sam Faiers

TOWIE star, 24, fronts bikini range for Very. And we mean “fronts”.

Lucy Mecklenburgh

THE Towie beauty, 23, is another making a splash with her own range of revealing swimwear.

Lorraine Kelly

THE columnist displayed her toned physique last week — and showed she is on the write lines at 55.
