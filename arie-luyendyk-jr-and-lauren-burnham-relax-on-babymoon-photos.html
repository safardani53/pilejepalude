Arie Luyendyk Jr. and Lauren Burnham Relax on Babymoon: Photos

Their shot at paradise! Arie Luyendyk Jr. and Lauren Burnham traveled to Bermuda in the weeks leading up to the arrival of their first child.

The Bachelor couple documented their gorgeous trip via their Instagram accounts, starting on Wednesday, April 17. Their vacation included private yoga sessions, kayaking, breathtaking views and plenty of mom and dad-to-be time.

Us Weekly exclusively revealed in November 2018 that the reality stars, who met during season 22 of The Bachelor, are expecting. “We weren’t trying, but we are so excited,” the 37-year-old former racecar driver told Us at the time. “It’s going to be a whole new chapter in our lives.”

Burnham, 27, shared an update on her pregnancy via Instagram in March. “I’m 30 weeks old and the size of a zucchini!” she wrote on her unborn child’s account. “Busy day in here, lemme tell ya. This morning Mom went for a check up and my little heart is beating strong at 150bpm. Did you know right now I have 20/400 vision? I’m still wearing some thick glasses in here, but my eyesight is getting better each day.”

She continued: “I’m getting bigger per usual and Mom has been too. She gained 3lbs in the last two weeks and we are both healthy as can be!”

Us exclusively broke the news in January that the pair have a baby girl on the way. The duo have not yet announced her name, though they are certain about it. “We’ve had her name picked out since Christmas!” Burnham wrote on her Instagram Story in March. “We think it’s beautiful and unique. We’ve never met anyone with her name.”
