Ashley Tisdale welcomes first child, daughter Jupiter Iris, with husband Chris French

ACTRESS Ashley Tisdale has welcomed her first child, daughter Jupiter Iris, with music composer husband Chris French.

The High School Musical Star announced her pregnancy in September with a sweet photo touching foreheads with her hubby.

Ashley, 35, posted a sweet photo on Instagram with the caption: "Jupiter Iris French arrived earth side 3.23.21."

In the black and white photo, she held hands with her newborn daughter.

The Disney Channel star shared the exciting news of her pregnancy this past Fall, in an adorable outdoor photoshoot while dressed in white.

The new mom cradled her baby bump as she smiled affectionately at her husband, Chris.

Later to her Instagram stories, she added that her spouse “is the hottest baby daddy.”

Ashley's friends and co-stars flooded the comments with their excitement, including High School Music alum Vanessa Hudgens who wrote: “Just the freaking cutest.”

Haylie Duff also gushed: “Been waiting for this!!! ✨✨✨✨ Can’t wait! Best mama & papa combo!”

The child star married her sweetheart in 2014 in a ceremony in Santa Barbara, California.

Chris is a music producer who composes for film and TV.

The couple first met in 2012 and were engaged just eight months later at the top of the Empire State Building.

On September 8th, the new parents celebrated their sixth wedding anniversary.

In October, Ashley and her husband revealed the gender of their newborn.

In a series of adorable photos, the pair cut into a tall white cake together to display a bright pink center.

Ashley wore a cute white and black polka dot dress as she gasped in surprise over the news.

The new mom captioned her photo set: “This year has obviously been hard. For so many. Ups and downs, and an emotional roller coaster. 

“I think this day was by far my favorite day EVER! I cried I was so happy.”

Ashley then spoke about the recent backlash against gender reveals, adding: “PS for all you people going above and beyond for gender reveal parties. A cake with the color inside is JUST as rewarding as fireworks but SAFER!”

The Suite Life of Zack & Cody star was not shy about her developing baby bump throughout her pregnancy, and in early February she posted her round tummy in a nude photo.

The actress posed naked in front of her mirror as she covered her face and breasts.

Her caption promoted body positivity, as she wrote: "So much of the time we give our love to others.

"Let’s start loving ourselves and our bodies in every shape and form.

"Our bodies do a lot for us every day and we neglect to acknowledge that."

"Thank your body and say I love you," she concluded.

Ashley also flaunted her developing figure in October when she wore a tight black catsuit for Halloween.

"Look at that bump, it's crazy," she remarked.

"I'm going to live in this for the rest of my pregnancy."
