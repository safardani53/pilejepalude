Baywatch star Kelly Rohrbach, 31, is pregnant and expecting baby boy with Walmart heir Steuart Walto

BAYWATCH movie star Kelly Rohrbach is reportedly pregnant with her first child.

The former Sports Illustrated model, who is married to Walmart heir Steuart Walton, is around eight months pregnant with a boy, sources told Page Six.

Kelly, 31, and Steuart, 40, even have a name picked out, but "are keeping it quiet,” according to the source.

The couple began dating around 2017, and married in 2019.

Steuart is the grandson of Walmart founder Sam Walton, and is on the board of the family business.

The 40-year-old is also a co-founder of holding company Runway Group, and is on the board of his family’s Crystal Bridges Museum of American Art, and the Smithsonian National Air and Space Museum.

When Kelly met and started dating Steuart in 2017, she was fresh from a relationship with Green Bay Packers quarterback Aaron Rodgers.

The model also reportedly dated Leonardo DiCaprio for six months in the summer of 2015.

Kelly played lifeguard CJ Parker in the 2017 big-screen Baywatch film, stepping into the tight red swimsuit originally worn by Pamela Anderson.

She has been mostly out of the public eye since marrying Steuart in June 2019.

They tied the knot in a secret ceremony in Florida after two years of dating.
