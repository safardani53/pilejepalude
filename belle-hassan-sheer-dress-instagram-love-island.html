Belle Hassan looks unrecognisable in barely-there sheer dress three years after Love Island

BELLE Hassan has been spotted looking unrecognisable in a barely-there sheer dress three years after starring on Love Island.

The reality star took to Instagram to pose for a sultry new snap as she showed off her incredible figure in a see-through outfit.

Love Island star Belle, 24, who has been holidaying in Ibiza this week, posted a series of snaps in the long see-through gown.

She paired the sparkling cover-up with a black bra and black knickers, accessorising with a designer waist bag and large black boots.

Bella finalised her look by straightening her short brunette hair, and opted for a glamorous palette of make-up, including a nude lipstick.

She posed holding onto a wall at a glamorous bar, before getting into the back of a taxi to pose for two more snaps on the way home.

"Unforgettable," she captioned the snap, as her fans and friends flooded the comments section to gush over her as "beautiful".

It comes after Belle hit out about this year's Love Island line-up, as she slammed them for looking "the same every year".

Belle asked her followers moments after the cast was released: "Anyone else think the love island cast gives 'I want to be an influencer'. Stunning... but boys and girls same every year man."

Elsewhere, Belle revealed she had been left nervous to go out after being attacked in a horrific unprovoked attack.
