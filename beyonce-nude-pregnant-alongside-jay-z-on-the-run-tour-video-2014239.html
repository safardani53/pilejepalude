Beyonce Poses Nude While Pregnant Alongside Jay Z in On the Run Tour

Any questions? Beyonce’s pregnancy with daughter Blue Ivy Carter, now 2,  has frequently been questioned by conspiracy theorists who claimed the pop diva faked her baby bump and used a surrogate or other means. But during her On the Run Tour HBO special, Bey, 33, silenced the critics when she aired some personal home videos. 

The Bonnie and Clyde theme of the joint tour featured cinematic video backdrops with the caption “This Is Not Real Life,” but when it came to the final duet of the night in their Paris, France concert, the mood shifted. Flashing “This Is Real Life” on the giant screens, the power couple proceeded to turn toward the screens singing a mash-up of Jay Z’s take on Alphaville’s 1984 hit “Forever Young” and Beyonce’s emotional 2008 anthem “Halo.” 

While the couple performed, home videos came up on the screen from when they first started dating back in 2002, to their 2008 wedding, to Beyonce’s pregnancy, to Blue Ivy’s first moments. 

Showing off her bare, pregnant belly, Bey revealed never-before-seen footage of her posing nude, with her arm covering her chest as her rapper hubby hugged her, holding her tummy from behind.
