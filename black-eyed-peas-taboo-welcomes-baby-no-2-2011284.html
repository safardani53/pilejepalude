Black Eyed Peas' Taboo Welcomes Third Son

There's another pea in the pod for Black Eyed Peas rapper Taboo!

The 35-year-old musician and his wife Jaymie Dizon welcomed their second child, a son, on Tuesday afternoon.

"The wifey is doing really good. This pregnancy is different from when we had Jalen," he told his Twitter followers. "I feel more focused and in control and not nervous at all."

(The couple's first child was born on July 19, 2009, just one year after Taboo and Dizon tied the knot.)

The Black Eyed Peas musician (real name: Jaime Gomez) live-tweeted his son's birth, documenting everything from his wife's epidural to her increasing dilation.

"I'm proud of my wife and honored to be by her side," he shared. "Baby Journey born 2:57 p.m. Yes! I am so excited!"
