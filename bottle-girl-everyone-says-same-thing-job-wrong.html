I'm a bottle girl - everyone says the same thing about my job, but they're wrong

A BOTTLE service waitress has revealed that her line of work is nowhere near easy, despite what people may say.

She said sometimes the nightlife-based job makes her lose sleep.

Kiko is an artist, bottle service girl, and digital content creator.

Her TikTok alone amassed over 37k followers and counting under the handle @kikoblac.

She took to the social media platform to share her work day woes.

"Them: being a bottle girl is easy," she wrote over a video of herself, parroting what she hears from people.

"Imagine how tired we are," a TikTok audio narrated in the background.

Kiko wore a semi-sheer army print bodysuit which showed off her dangerous curves.

"Haven't had a full 8hrs of sleep since Thursday," she wrote in the caption. "I'm running on Redbull and hookah."

Viewers shared their two cents regarding Kiko's work-life balance in the comment section of the video.

Some trolls mocked Kiko's message, suggesting that the job isn't difficult.

"Wowwwww sounds soooo hardd," a hater sarcastically wrote.

"She like brings out the bottles to patrons at strip clubs," another troll commented. "Literally the easiest job."

Others supported Kiko, and gave her words of encouragement.

"If you work at a bar this is hell week," one affirmed.

"The worst is when they order the cheapest bottles or just waste ur time asking for prices and don’t buy anything," another added.

"Every job look 'easy' until you have to do it," a commenter wrote.
