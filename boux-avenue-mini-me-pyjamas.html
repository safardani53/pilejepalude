Boux Avenue's Mini Me Pyjamas are back for Christmas and we're obsessed

BOUX Avenue has dropped their latest Mini Me matching pyjama collection in time for Christmas - and it's perfect timing if you ask us.

The new Mini Me collection has returned for another year after being a huge hit with shoppers in previous years.

The Mini Me matching pyjama collection is available to buy online and in store now from Boux Avenue.

Boux Avenue has heard our cries for matching pyjama sets, which have become a huge trend in recent years.

If you want to get the whole family in on the co-ordinating look for an unmissable picture opportunity on Christmas Day makes for even more fun, and it's even easier thanks to Boux Avenue.

The Mini Me collection is back by popular demand, with one pair in particular being highly sought after.

According to Boux Avenue sales for the gingerbread matching PJ set has increased by over 200% in the last week, and it is top of our wish list this Christmas too.

So you'll need to be fast if you want these.

The leading lingerie and nightwear brand's Mini Me collection includes a whole host of pyjama sets for children and adults, which makes a total of 16 items in the collection, ranging from children's ages 2 to 12 and up to adult dress sizes 6 to 18.

The range features eight different designs for adults and children all with a festive theme; from miniature gingerbreads emblazoned on the front, festive giraffes, to Fairisle and star print creations.

Boux's Mini Me collection ranges in price from £14 to £28 for children's nightwear, and £30 to £40 for the adult alternative.
