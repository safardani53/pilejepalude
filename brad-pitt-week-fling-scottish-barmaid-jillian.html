DETAILS of Brad Pitt's week-long fling with a Scottish barmaid called Jillian back in 1994 have been unearthed in a resurfaced interview.

The 56-year-old hunk fell for the Glasgow-based beauty during a visit to the city in the '90s, calling her a "lovely girl".

In 2000, the Oscar-winning star is said to have refused to do any interviews apart from one with a Scottish The Daily Record reporter at the premiere for Guy Ritchie movie Snatch.

It was then that he recalled the details of his brief romance with Jillian Lamb, who he first met at Danny Kane's pub on Mitchell street in 1994, before the romance moved to The Mariott Hotel and One Devonshire Gardens.

Brad also told them about nights out in local nightclub Tunnel - and even recounted enjoying homemade soap while he and Jillian babysat her niece at her parent's house.

Brad told the publication: "Jillian was a lovely girl", with the British barmaid admitting in a 2007 interview that she thought Brad "looked a bit weird" when she first saw him.

She explained: "I was working in Danny Kane's pub in the morning and I became aware of someone looking in the window at me.

"Two guys came in and sat down and you could tell right away they were a bit different. Brad had asked my name from another bar worker and was shouting over at me."

She added: "Being honest, I didn't remotely fancy him at first. I thought he looked a bit weird."

Jillian added that he struggled to pronounce her name at first due to their differing accents - and after fighting off attention from starstruck fans he told her: "You're the only girl I am interested in."

The Sun Online has contacted Brad's rep for further comment.

Jillian joins a bevy of A-list beauties Brad has enjoyed romances with - including Gwyneth Paltrow, Jennifer Aniston, and Angelina Jolie.

His love life has been under constant scrutiny throughout his time in the spotlight, with the star even joking about his relationship failures when he accepted a SAG Award last month.

Holding his statue, Brad quipped: "I have to add this to my Tinder profile."

He also poked fun at his marriages while describing the character he played in Once Upon A Time In Hollywood, saying: "Let’s be honest; [Cliff Booth] was a difficult part: a guy who gets high, takes his shirt off and doesn’t get on with his wife. It was a big stretch."
