Carlie Becker: Cheerleading Coach Fired for Playboy Photos

Carlie Becker, a.k.a. Carlie Christine, was the cheerleading coach for Casa Robles High School in Orangevale, CA, until Playboy photos of her were leaked onto the internet.

Carlie was outed by a group of girls angry about being kicked off the cheerleading squad. The cheerleaders had skipped school, prompting their dismissal, and subsequently their “revenge” on the coach.

Carlie was dismissed from her job when school officials learned of the nude photos.
