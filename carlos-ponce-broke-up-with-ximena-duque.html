Carlos Ponce Broke Up with Ximena Duque, Know about his married life and affairs

The famous actor and TV host Carlos Ponce and the actress from Colombia Ximena Duque are no longer together with each other. After spending several years of togetherness they finally decided to get apart from each other. Yes, we know it has been long since this pair has been detached and living apart from one another but we just thought to relive past memories.

This couple dated for almost six years and no one ever thought that they would ever give up their relationship. Many of their fans were actually shocked to hear this news as they were always a very admired and loved couple. So who is Carlos having an affair with? Is he living a married life or is he single?

Carlos Ponce and Ximena Duque's breakup story

Carlos Ponce and Ximena Duque stayed as a couple for six years and were in a live-in relationship for three years. But their attachment faded out at the end of the year 2015. Fans were very surprised by their decision to get separated and when the fans asked millions of questions, the actress gave a simple answer.

Ximena confessed that she walks according to her religious law and it doesn’t allow and accept a woman to live with someone without getting married to that person.

"As a woman and as a mother and as a Christian, I should've never left my house without getting married first"

In the joint statement of Carlos and Ximena, they said, 

"We’ve had some wonderful, special times during these years together. We went through different stages and in each one we got to know each other, respect each other and appreciate each other more. And like everything in life, our love has changed and has taken another turn – to friendship",

As their fans always supported their togetherness, the couple thanked the fans and even the press and public. They also mentioned how everyone has not only supported them as a couple but, also in their career. There was also a request done by this couple to support them further as they are now moving in their separate ways in their new personal and professional life.
