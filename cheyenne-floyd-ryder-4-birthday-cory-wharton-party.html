TEEN Mom Cheyenne Floyd reunited with her ex Cory Wharton to celebrate her daughter Ryder's fourth birthday in an epic bash.

Celebrations hosted by the soon-to-be mother of two, who has been busy setting up Ryder's "big girl" bedroom and the nursery for her unborn son, also included Cory's girlfriend Taylor Selfridge and her 11-month-old Mila.

For the birthday celebration, Teen Mom star Cheyenne rented out a play space for the kids to play safely as they mounted tiny carousels.

Cheyenne filmed the lavish affair on her Instagram stories, showing fans around the set up, which included a small soft play area.

In another clip from the bash, Ryder could be seen riding the carousel before jumping out of it and dancing for the camera.

The 4-year-old wore a blue tutu with black biker shorts and white tee that had her name written on it. She topped off the look with a blue bow on her hair.

If that wasn't enough of a celebration, Cheyenne then went on to throw her daughter a second, more private affair at her home on Ryder's actual birthday, April 7.

The more private affair was for just the immediate family, as Ryder was pictured with her mom and Zach, as well as Cheyenne's blossoming bump.

A wall of balloons had been set up in the back yard in front of a child-sized table and chairs for the perfect photo opportunity.

Cheyenne said the children passed time by "making slime" and spending time in the sunshine getting "glitter tattoos".
