Elizabeth Olsen: Engaged to Boyd Holbrook!

With Mary-Kate Olsen engaged to Olivier Sarkozy, this famous family can now start thinking about a double wedding.

Because Elizabeth Olsen is engaged as well!

Us Weekly confirms that the actress has agreed to exchange vows with actor Boyd Holbrook.

"They’ve been by each other for a while, taking a lot of couple time," an insider tells the tabloid, adding that the two have been "talking about getting married" for awhile now.

 

They recently moved in together in Brooklyn.

Olsen and Holbrook have been dating since they met on the set of the film Very Good Girls in 2012.

Mary-Kate, 27, and her French fiance, 44, are set to get married and are "talking about trying to get pregnant," a source claims, while Ashley Olsen is now dating Moneyball director Bennett Miller.

We send everyone in this family our very best wishes!
