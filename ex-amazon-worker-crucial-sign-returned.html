I'm an ex-Amazon worker - lookout for a crucial sign to see if you've been sent a returned package

A FORMER worker at Amazon has revealed a key detail that will let you know if you received a package that's been returned before.

Customers should be able to find the telltale sign on their package from the e-commerce giant.

It should be in the form of a barcode, according to a recent video from TikToker and alleged former employee, Caytee, who goes by the username lilbitofevrythng.

Caytee showed an Amazon package in the clip and pointed to a barcode with the letters "LPN" on it.

"If you get an item with this LPN sticker on the item or on the packaging, it's been bought and returned to Amazon," she explained.

"And then it's been re-sold to you," she continued.

Some felt it was wrong for Amazon to re-send returned packages, while others professed it would've been a waste to throw them out.

"The last several packages I've ordered have had that sticker on it," one TikToker noted.
