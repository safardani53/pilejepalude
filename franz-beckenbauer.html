Franz Beckenbauer Biography

Franz  Beckenbauer started his career in 1965, playing for German Bundesliga and became famous for his role of the team's sweeper. Starting out playing as a midfielder he later established himself as the central defender. Beckenbauer who has been able to lift world cup both as the team captain and as the team's coach is regarded as the world most versatile player of the century. Besides his name is also listed in the FIFA 100 of the world's greatest living players. 

Franz Beckenbauer is a German by nationality and belongs to white ethnicity. Regarding his personal life, he has been married three times and has five children from his all three wives. After a divorce from his first wife, Brigitte Beckenbauer, and his second wife Sybille Beckenbauer, he is currently in a married relationship with his third wife Heidi Burmester. Moving on to the economic aspect of Franz, he is regarded as one of the richest German footballers. Currently, at the age of 71, Beckenbauer in his career as a football player earns a net worth of around U.S $10 million.
