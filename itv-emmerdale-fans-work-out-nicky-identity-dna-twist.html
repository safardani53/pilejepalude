ITV Emmerdale fans 'work out' Nicky's real identity in Kim Tate DNA 'twist'

ITV Emmerdale fans have 'worked out' Nicky's real identity in a Kim Tate DNA 'twist'.

The character arrived in Emmerdale earlier this year to look after the kids at Home Farm.

One fan shared their theory online, writing: "Probably overthinking it but a good indicator there is that something is up with Caleb & Nicky is that Nicky has been in the show for months and strangely no last name???

"Thas has me thinking he is related to someone already in the village… me thinks Kim #Emmerdale."
