Who is Jason Lewis?

Jason Lewis was born on June 25, 1971, in Newport Beach, California.

Jason is an actor who is best known for his role in the HBO series, Sex and the City, where he starred as Jerry Jarrod.

Additionally, he has appeared in other hit films such as My Bollywood Bride, Mr. Brook, and the drama television series Beverly Hills, 90210.
