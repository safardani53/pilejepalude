Movie Actress Jeon Do-yeon was born in Seoul, South Korea on February 11, 1973. She's 50 years old today.

South Korean actress who is known for her roles in films including the 1997 romantic drama The Contact, 2013 drama Way Back Home, 2015 action film Memories of the Sword. She has also starred in the television shows Shoot for the Stars and Lovers in Prague.
