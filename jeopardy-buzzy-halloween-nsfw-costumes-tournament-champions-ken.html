Jeopardy!'s Buzzy Cohen urges fans to watch Halloween episode in NSFW costumes &amp; reveals hilarious p

JEOPARDY! star Buzzy Cohen has urged fans at home to dress up in NSFW costumes while watching the game show's upcoming Halloween episode, and send their looks to him.

He and another exec promised a wild prize for the best one involving host Ken Jennings, 48.

Season 39 of Jeopardy! is hitting the small screen in just days, premiering on September 12th.

When last season ended, former champ Ken Jennings, 48, and actress Mayim Bialik, 46, were finally announced as the permanent successors for the late, great host Alex Trebek.

In the offseason, the treasured trivia show has been keeping fans excited with rare interviews from its game show greats on their new weekly podcast Inside Jeopardy!.

On this week's episode, however, alum Buzzy, instead unleashed his hellishly hilarious plans for October 31st's episode.

Alongside podcast co-host, producer and former "Clue Crew" member Sarah Foss, Buzzy - who won nine games in 2016 and the 2017 $250K Tournament of Champions - revealed:

"The Tournament of Champions day one is going to be on Halloween.

"I want everyone to dress up in their favorite Jeopardy!-themed Halloween-themed costume, post a picture hashtagged #DeathlyDouble.

"And a committee selected by me will choose who had the best costume and get an exclusive, special Jeopardy! prize."

'SIGNED PHOTO OF KEN'

Buzzy revealed that grand prize would be: "A one-of-a-kind photo of Ken Jennings signed by Buzzy Cohen," he said.

"Obviously you can dress up as a contestant, as your favorite host ... but get creative."

He then started pitching ideas: "Maybe a sexy Daily Double?"

"How about your dog as Alex Tre-Bark, maybe?"

Co-host Sarah burst out laughing but was game to commit; she'll dress up in "full Marie Antoinette" she said.

"I love it" a slightly bewildered Sarah concluded.

Buzzy then confirmed he was deathly serious after the podcast aired and tweeted: "Folks. Prep your Jeopardy-themed costume and post it with #DeathlyDouble for a chance to win an incredible prize!"

One enthralled fan replied: "I'M LITERALLY DEAD."

Another wrote: "I'm going as Buzzy Cohen."

JEOPARDY!'S FAVORITE HOLIDAY?

Jeopardy! loves to have a little fun when Halloween rears its head.

In 2017, the show tweeted a photo of the late host Alex Trebek done up in a virtual and peculiar pirate costume while he was still host.

The show lovingly joked: "Alex dressed up as himself for Halloween (in an orange tie)! What did you think of his costume?"

Indeed, there was Alex with a fake eye patch and parrot.

The game show’s social media team also got creative when re-airing their episode from Halloween 2021 this summer.

The original photo of each player competing at the time was tweeted out and altered in the spirit of the holiday for the re-run.

Then-champ Tyler Rhode scored some new fangs, newcomer When Romero had ghosts coming out of her image and in the center was contestant Emily Robinson with a full Phantom mask covering her face.

Emily - a brunette teacher from Brooklyn - was not expecting this depiction.

She quote-tweeted the official image and wrote: "Desperate to know who on the @Jeopardy social media team made the choice to turn me into the Phantom of the Opera."

Though she added with a positive pluck: "This is ART."

SEASON 39

Ken and Mayim began filling in for the legendary Alex after his tragic 2020 passing at age 80 and a whirlwind of Season 37 celebrity guest hosts.

The lead-up to the final decision announced last month had feverish fans picking sides - largely viewers have preferred Ken in online polls - in a heated 2-year category of pop culture discussion. 

They included Savannah Guthrie, LeVar Burton, Dr. Oz, and Anderson Cooper.

Many fans wanted the well-dressed bespectacled Buzzy to get a chance but he never did - many tweet #hirebuzzy alongside their Jeopardy! Twitter takes to this day.

Earlier guest hosts included former executive producer Mike Richards who even scored the host gig from the inside before it was "reversed."

When the show returns, Ken will host first and Mayim will host from January on.
