What to do when your relevance as a star is tied to your cup size? Upgrade!

This prompted the peanut gallery to step forward.

"She’s terrified that she’s become irrelevant,” a source close to Simpson told Radar.  “She hates the fact that the Kardashians and others are getting all the attention she used to own."
