Jessica Simpson Posts Thirsty Swimsuit Photo

Jessica Simpson is "thirsty" for some warmer weather this winter!

The singer, 43, shared an Instagram photo of herself modeling a zebra-print swimsuit from her clothing line on Thursday, writing in the caption, "THIRSTY."

Simpson poses against a wall while sporting the Eazy Zebra Plunge One Piece from the Jessica Simpson Collection. Simpson regained control of the company after she bought it from partner Sequential Brands Group in 2021. 

The fashion mogul, who was dripped in jewels, wore a pearl choker, a dainty silver choker, a gold necklace with a diamond pendant of a tiger’s head, a single silver bracelet and a pair of large bejeweled silver hoop earrings. She accessorized her look with a pair of bronze metallic pointed high heels and oversized aviator sunglasses.

The Employee of the Month actress completed her look with a dark brown smokey eyeshadow look and a glossy nude lip.

Simpson recently opened up about sticking to her fashion “staples” from the Y2K era.

“I feel like I've never really left that style,” the musician told PEOPLE exclusively on the red carpet at the 2023 Footwear News Achievement Awards in New York City. “I always go back to a platform when it comes to a shoe. I have my staples and they end up being a little Y2K-[inspired].” 

At the event in which Simpson was honored with the Icon Award, she wore a shimmering floor-length silver sheer dress.

During an October chat about a recent Jessica Simpson Collection drop, Simpson revealed her daughters Maxwell "Maxi" Drew, 11, and Birdie Mae, 4, have been exploring her many looks.

"I have to do a weekly girls' closet inventory to check for missing items! They even know my hiding spots somehow,” Simpson told PEOPLE. She joked that she may have to consider “having one closet for all three of us ladies.” 

Simpson then revealed that her girls excel in an area that she does not.

“Maxwell and Birdie are WAY more organized than I will ever be in that department,” Simpson said. “I have a coat closet, but that is about it for how I seasonally manage my wardrobe.”
