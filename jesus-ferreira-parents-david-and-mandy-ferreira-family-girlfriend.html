Jesus Ferreira Parents David &amp; Mandy Ferreira- Family &amp; Girlfriend

MLS soccer player Jesus Ferreira parents, David and Mandy Ferreira, are his primary source of inspiration and support.

With his parents right by his side, the athlete receives more strength and courage. As his father is from an athletic background, it would not be wrong to state that Jesus Ferreira has followed in his footsteps. 

The Colombian-born soccer player plays in the forward position for Major League Soccer (MLS) Club FC Dallas. 

Jesus made his career club debut in 2017, scoring his first professional goal against Real Salt Lake. In 2018, he moved on loan to Tulsa Roughnecks, where he six goals in 14 games, including his first career hat-trick. 

Later, the player rejoined FC Dallas and established himself as the club’s leading goal-scorer. With his impressive agility and speed, Jesus has gained worldwide fame and received numerous awards. 

His notable titles include the 2022 MLS Young Player of the Year and the 2022 MLS Best XI.

Internationally, Jesus has also contributed to the national team of the United States since February 2020. During the 2022 CONCACAF Nations League A campaign against Grenada, Jesus scored four goals in a 5-0 victory for his team. 

The young champ excels in his professional career, competing nationally and internationally. 

Jesus Ferreira Parents: David Ferreira & Mandy Ferreira

The 22-year-old player is one of three children of his parents, David Ferreira and Mandy Ferreira. 

His father, David (born: August 9, 1979), a former Colombian footballer, has a strong background in sports.

From 1997 to 2019, he pursued a professional football career by playing for several teams.

During his playing career, David led his teams toward multiple victories, winning several accolades.

As an international player himself, Jesus’s father played for the Colombian national team from 2001 to 2008. 

In 2009, David joined FC Dallas, the team his son, Jesus, now plays for. While at FC Dallas, he earned the MLS MVP title and made it into the 2010 MLS Best XI list. 

Likewise, his mother, Mandy, has been by his side since his early days, supporting and encouraging him to achieve more. Jesus acknowledges his mother’s sacrifices as the reason for his success. Further details regarding her are yet to be out. 

Additionally, the player grew up in Santa Marta alongside his two brothers: Santiago and Matthew Ferreira. His brothers are also active in sports, and they take him as their inspiration.
