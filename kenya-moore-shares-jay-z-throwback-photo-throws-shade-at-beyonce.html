Kenya Moore Shares Jay-Z Throwback Photo, Throws Shade at Beyonce

When is a Throwback Thursday photo not just a Throwback Thursday photo?

When it appears to pour fuel on to the fire of an alleged divorce scandal that centers on one of Hollywood’s most powerful couples.

Earlier this week, we reported on the rumor that Jay-Z had moved out and that his marriage to Beyonce was in serious trouble.

We have no idea whether or not this piece of intriguing chatter is true or not, but Kenya Moore appears to have jumped all over it for some reason.

A day after the break-up rumor went viral, Moore shared the above #TBT picture on Instagram.

She also included a caption with it that seemed to throw a bit of shade at Beyonce.

"On set of #moneyaintathang with Jay-Z circa 1998 #happytoworkwithhim such a gentleman and talented rapper turned mogul," she wrote, adding:

"#booked #ineedanewbentley #funtimes #setlife. Wish @beyonce was there."

Is The Real Housewives of Atlanta cast member actually flirting with Jay-Z here?

Is she actually taunting Queen Bey?

One would think not because, come on… who dares to cross the Queen and her man?

But how else would you interpet the timing of this posting and the message affiliated with it?

Tread carefully here, Kenya. That’s all we’re gonna say.
