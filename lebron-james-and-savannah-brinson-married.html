LeBron James and Savannah Brinson: Married!

Savannah Brinson and LeBron James have tied the knot.

The couple, parents of two sons and sweethearts for the past 12 (!) years since, finally made it official at the Grand Del Mar hotel in San Diego, Calif.

The couple’s sons LeBron James, Jr., 8, and Bryce Maximus James, 6, were there for mom and dad’s big day along with friends and family members.

On hand for the wedding were LeBron’s teammate and fellow NBA all-star Dwyane Wade, his girlfriend Gabrielle Union, singer Ne-Yo and many others.

LeBron James and Savannah Brinson got engaged in January 2012 after he proposed with a huge ring at the Shelborne hotel in South Beach.

Congratulations to the happy couple and family!
