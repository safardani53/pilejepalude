Lisa Snowdon bears an uncanny resemblance to Kate Middleton as she arrives at London Fashion Week

SHE might be considered presenting royalty, but Lisa Snowdon probably never thought she’d be compared to ACTUAL royalty – until today that is.

Because the former radio host bore an uncanny resemblance to the Duchess of Cambridge today as she arrived for an event as part of London Fashion Week.

Lisa, 45, was snapped arriving at the show for fashion label PPQ dressed in black trousers and spotted cold shoulder blouse.

She teamed the outfit with towering heels and a bag but it was her demure smile and shiny dark locks that made onlookers double-take that she wasn’t Miss Middleton.

Lisa also took to her Instagram page to post a snap with her friend Aicha McKenzie, the CEO of AMCK Model and AMCK Dance Agency.

The pair of them both sported polka dots on their tops and Lisa captioned it: “It's a spotty thang tonight with @aichamckenzie @PPQLondon #lfw.”

And it’s little wonder Lisa couldn’t wipe the grin off her face having now found happiness in her love life.

Last month, she announced she was engaged to boyfriend George Smart after he got down on one knee at their home in Essex.

Speaking exclusively to Fabulous magazine, she said: “George proposed on the Saturday before Christmas and it was such a lovely surprise – I had absolutely no idea he was going to do it!
