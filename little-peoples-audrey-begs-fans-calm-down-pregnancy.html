Little People's Audrey Roloff begs fans to 'calm down' after she sparks pregnancy rumors with new po

AUDREY Roloff has clapped back at fan pregnancy rumors after sparking baby speculation with a new post.

The Little People Big World star has raised eyebrows lately in her recent posts as many think she might have a new little one on the way.

But Audrey, 31, seemingly shut down all rumors after replying to fan questions on her Instagram on Tuesday.

The reality star took to the platform to hold a Q&A with her followers.

After one asked: "What supplements are you taking? From where?" she replied: "Thanks for reminding me!" with a winky face.

"I put them in my pocket so I wouldn't forget after I ate. I take different things depending on the day and how I'm feeling or what's going on that week (if we're traveling, need extra immune support, on my period, tired, etc.)

PREGNANCY CLUES?

Earlier this month, rumors spread that Audrey is pregnant with her fourth child after she dropped two major hints on her social media.

The mother of three took to her Stories on March 17 to finally answer a follower Q&A she had previously hosted.

After one fan asked: "Why did you make the switch to decaf? Notice any changes??" the TV personality explained how she's cut both caffeinated coffee and alcohol out of her life.

"I've been doing it since Lent started. I have been wanting to try going off for a while to see how I feel.

"I've been drinking coffee since college and also drank it through all my pregnancies and postpartum (with the exception of the first few weeks of pregnancy when it sounded repulsive to me) so far I've noticed my heart being less pounding feeling and I also feel less panicky and 'hurried' in the mornings."

Audrey continued: "And my Oura ring says my sleep has been better too but that could be some other things too.

"I also haven't drank alcohol in a couple of months and I've been running a lot more the last couple months too," she admitted.
