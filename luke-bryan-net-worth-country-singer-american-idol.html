What is Luke Bryan's net worth?

LUKE Bryan is a huge country star with an impressive list of hits and awards to his name.

He has been a judge on American Idol since 2018 and is set to go out on tour later in 2022.

Who is Luke Bryan?

Thomas Luther “Luke” Bryan was born on July 17, 1976, in Leesburg, Georgia.

He began his music career writing songs for Travis Tritt and Billy Currington before signing his own deal with Capitol Nashville in 2007.

Bryan was intending to move to Nashville when he was 19 but his plan was derailed due to a family tragedy when his older brother Chris was tragically killed in a car accident.

Instead, he went to college in Georgia and graduated in business administration in 1999.

He eventually made it to Nashville two years later and started out as a songwriter but soon signed up as a performer, gaining his first success with All My Friends Say.

Since then he has picked up hit after hit and released a stack of successful albums.

LUKE BRYAN

His first ten albums contained 27 number one hits.

In 2013, Bryan was named "Entertainer of the Year" by both the Academy of Country Music Awards and the Country Music Association

Over the years he has sold more than 75 million records.

He became a judge on American Idol in 2018.

Bryan is married to Caroline Boyer.

The pair wed on December 8, 2006, and have two sons together, Thomas Boyer "Bo", born March 18, 2008, and Tatum "Tate" Christopher, born August 11, 2010.

The couple adopted Luke's nephew and nieces, Jordan, Kris, and Til, after their parents both tragically passed away

Luke's sister Kelly died suddenly in 2007 and tragedy struck again in 2014 when Kelly's husband Ben Lee Cheshire also passed away, leaving their three children parentless.

What is Luke Bryan's net worth?

Bryan is estimated to have a net worth of $160m, according to Celebrity Net Worth.

He is considered to be one of the highest paid artists in the world.

The site states that between June 2019 and June 2020 he earned $45m.

It’s reported he earns $1m per show and $12m per year for appearing on American Idol.

The Richest though puts his net worth at a much more modest $47m.
