Mark Wahlberg's Wife Rhea Gushes Over His Calvin Klein Ad

#Aintnuthingliketherealthingbaby! Mark Wahlberg’s wife Rhea still thinks her husband’s original 1992 Calvin Klein ad is the best. The mom of four took to Twitter on Tuesday, Jan. 6, and offered her thoughts on Justin Bieber’s new ad, in which he mimics Marky Mark by posing in just his underwear.

“Just gonna say this…..did u ever hear the song #aintnuthinliketherealthingbaby,” Rhea tweeted, before adding, “My baby was the 1st and the best. And not to mention……wow the retouching.”

Just gonna say this…..did u ever hear the song #aintnuthinliketherealthingbaby ????

— Rhea W. (@rheadur) January 7, 2015

Wahlberg’s ladylove even took a moment to reminisce her hubby’s younger years. “Mark wahlberg calvin klein ads – Google Search that’s the real…..ain’t gonna lie,” she tweeted, along with a link to the google image search results for her husband’s ad.

While Rhea likely meant no harm, Bieber’s fans still came to his defense, tweeting at Rhea, to which she responded: “No hate. Just saying the original was the best that’s all.”

Bieber, for his part, seems to have gotten Wahlberg’s approval. “Just spoke to my boy @mark_wahlberg. Honored to be a part of the legacy. @CalvinKlein #mycalvins” he tweeted later that day.
