Mel B Earns Perfect Score with Rousing Rumba on Dancing with the Stars (Video)

Scary Spice Mel B and her dance partner Maksim Chmerkovskiy received their first perfect score dancing the rumba in week 6 of competition on “Dancing with the Stars.”

Ironically Mel and Maks only trained six hours this week, while the other contestants trained an average of 20 hours. The love/hate vibe of the rumba seemed to be right up their alley.

The results show airs tonight at 9/8c on ABC.
