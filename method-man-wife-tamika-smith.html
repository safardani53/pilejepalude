Who is Method Man's wife Tamika Smith?

METHOD Man is a member of one of the most influential music groups in history, the Wu-Tang Clan.

The rapper, who's also been very successful in TV and film, is married to Tamika Smith.

Who is Method Man?

Method Man is a rapper, songwriter, record producer and actor, and was born in New York on March 2, 1971.

The 49-year-old, real name Clifford Smith, is best known for his time in the East Coast hip hop group Wu-Tang Clan, who have released four gold and platinum studio albums.

In 1996, Smith won a Grammy Award for Best Rap Performance by a Duo or Group, for I'll Be There for You/You're All I Need to Get By, with Mary J. Blige.

Method has also made waves on both the small and big screen, appearing in films How High, Red Tails and The Cobbler, and he can currently be seen on Starz's Power Book II, as lawyer Davis McClean.

What happened between Wendy Williams and Method Man?

Talk show host Wendy Williams said she had a "one-night stand" with Method Man during a wild night of "smoking weed and giving him a bath."

In an interview with DJ Suss One. she recalled the night she claimed she spent with the rapper at her penthouse.

Williams, 56, explained: "I smoked a blunt with Method Man while I gave him a bath and it was a one-night-stand. And he'll deny it, maybe not.

"It was one night, we were in the club when a fight broke out and gunshots broke out.

"He rolled a blunt, we smoked a blunt, we watched the fight, we heard the gunshots, the cops came in.

"He was rolling with the car from Staten Island with the whole Clan and their people and I guess I batted my eyes and rocked my shoulder, you know how I do, and I said, 'you wanna come over?' And he said, 'yeah.'"

Who is Method Man's wife Tamika Smith?

Method Man and Tamika Smith got engaged in 1999 and married two years later in 2001.

The pair met while Tamika was his personal assistant between 1997 and 2002.

The actor's leading lady lives a very private life and he's shielded her from the media.

Tamika is a survivor of breast cancer and Wendy Williams revealed the news in 2006, which angered Method as he did not want to make his wife's diagnosis public. She is now cancer free.
