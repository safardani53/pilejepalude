Modern Family's youngest son Nolan Gould shocks fans by dancing and twerking on stripper pole

MODERN Family actor Nolan Gould dubbed himself "tragic Mike" after writhing around in his first pole dancing class.

Known to fans of the sitcom as super-cute Luke Dunphy, the youngest sibling of the Dunphy clan, the New Yorker proved all grown up in his recent lesson.

Nolan, 22, captured the action on his Instagram, with a video showing him wearing gray shorts and a matching top and holding the pole with one hand.

He swung himself around before holding the pole with both hands for some hip circles, before flipping himself around to slide down for a deep squat.

The clip wrapped with the TV star wrapping both feet around the bar for an impressive twirl.

In his caption, the ABC star wrote: "Tragic Mike. Might delete this immediately but had to share the results of my first pole dancing class.

"Always wanted to try it. Thanks @alyssadventures for an amazing class and my supportive friends for helping make it happen.

"I am uncoordinated and inflexible as hell. My body is basically made of Lego bricks."
