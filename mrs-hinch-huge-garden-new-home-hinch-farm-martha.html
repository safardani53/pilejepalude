Mrs Hinch shows off the huge garden at her new home Hinch Farm as she welcomes Martha in

MRS Hinch has splashed out on a stunning five-bedroom Essex farmhouse that she has loved since she was 11 years old.

Taking to her Instagram, the cleaning whizz, 31, showed off the huge grounds as she rode on a new sit-on lawnmower that she has called ‘Martha.’

Mrs Hinch revealed she has called her new countryside mansion Hinch Farm, and called herself a “farm girl” as she rode on “Martha”.

She showed off a photo of the beautiful large grounds and said: “This picture gives me goosebumps because I took this the day I went to view the house for the first time.

“This is the lawn at its best.

“And the lines in the grass made me smile because they remind me of my hoover lines.

“P.S. I’ve called her Martha. Martha the mower. Welcome to the fam.”

On New Year’s Day, she showed how she had collected the keys with husband Jamie and gave fans a tour around the swanky new digs.

In the clip, she said: “So me and Jamie are on our way now to pick up some very, very special keys.

“I don't even know what to say if I'm honest, I feel really numb but I feel really excited, there it is. Here we go!”

The beautiful home features a grand wooden staircase, stables and sprawling grounds.

Sophie, who gave birth to second son Lennie in May, 2021, showed off her eldest boy Ronnie, two, happily exploring his new home.

Perfect for the boys, the pad comes with a garden playhouse and horses in a neighbouring field.

Mrs Hinch revealed the story behind the home, and explained: "Truth is my parents and I have been driving past this house since I was 11.

"And we always said to each other, we wonder who lives in there, we even used to imagine here and what we could do if we ever did."

Mrs Hinch could be seen celebrating her new house purchase by nailing a Hinch Farm sign onto the fence.

In a caption alongside the post, she penned: “It's time to write a new chapter in our story. 

“This still doesn't feel real and we will never be able to thank you all enough because without our crazy little insta world this wouldn't be happening. 

“I can't wait to take you all along with us!! welcome to Hinch Farm everyone.”
