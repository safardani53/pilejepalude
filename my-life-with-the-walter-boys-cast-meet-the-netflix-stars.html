‘My Life With the Walter Boys’ is now out on Netflix. Check out photos of the cast members, including the adorable newcomers.

Netflix continues to release new content, and My Life With the Walter Boys is the family drama you’re going to watch this December. The new series dropped on December 7 and follows the journey of Jackie Howard as she picks up her life in the wake of tragedy.

The show features several new faces, including Nikki Rodriguez as Jackie, Noah LaLonde as Cole, and more. Suits alum Sarah Rafferty also stars alongside Marc Blucas. My Life With the Walter Boys is based on the Wattpad novel by Ali Novak. Scroll through our gallery to see photos of the My Life With the Walter Boys cast.
