'Naked and Afraid' Latest News and Updates

In the Discovery Channel reality TV series Naked and Afraid, two strangers are left alone in the wilderness with no clothes, food, water, or shelter for 21 days. They are allowed to bring one helpful item with them, like a fire starter or a machete. Other than that, they're on their own. (Well, except for the camera crew and producers.)

Network: Discovery Channel

First Episode Date: June 23, 2013

Is Naked and Afraid real?

Naked and Afraid is real in the sense that the survivalists don't have any food, shelter, or clothing, and they're expected to survive for three weeks on their own. However, it's worth noting the production crew is literally following them around, and they're never completely alone in the case of an emergency. Not to mention, the footage is edited after the fact. There have also been reports of producers giving one of the survivalists a tampon when she was on her period. So you can decide for yourself if that makes it any less "real."

Where to Watch: Discovery Channel, Discovery Plus
