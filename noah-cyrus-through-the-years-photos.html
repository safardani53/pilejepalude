Noah Cyrus, born January 8, 2000, is a singer and younger sister of Miley Cyrus. The young star was born into a very famous family, but over the years she has paved her own way. Noah’s career really took off in 2016 when her song “Make Me (Cry)” featuring Labrinth came out. 

Noah has subsequently released two albums. Her 2018 album was titled “Good Cry” and her 2020 album was titled “The End Of Everything.” Noah’s work eventually led to her earning a Grammy nomination for Best New Artist! Fans have followed Noah’s career for a few years now, and cannot wait to see what she accomplishes in the future!
