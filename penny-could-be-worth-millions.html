I'm a coin collector - a 1974 penny could be worth $2million - see the exact details to look for

A COIN collector recently shared his knowledge about a penny that could be worth millions.

Tiktok user TheCoinChannel, whose real first name is Eric, showed what to look for on pennies to know if you've struck gold.

The influencer uses his platform to educate his over 4.2million followers on different rare coins.

Eric said the penny in question is worth an estimated $2million.

However, despite its high value, the penny is technically illegal to own as it's considered to be government property.

Federal authorities haven't been in a hurry to confiscate them, according to Coinworld, but it's important for sellers to know.

According to Eric, the penny was part of an experiment done by the US Mint in the 1970s to bring down production costs of pennies.

To do this, it tried using aluminum to make pennies instead of copper.

In total, 1.5million pennies were made using aluminum during this time.

However the experiment did not work out as planned, so all of the aluminum pennies were supposed to be destroyed.

Despite that, some collectors were able to hang onto theirs.

Eric shared the story of a man who sold one of these special pennies to a collector, only to have the government take them to court over the coin.

Eric said the coin community estimates that 13 or 14 of these pennies are still in circulation.

To potentially spot one, which is unlikely, you'll need to look out for the 1974 mintage year and check the metal of the piece.

How to know if your coin is valuable

Checking through your spare change for any rare nickels, half dollars, dimes, and others might just lead to the discovery of a rare coin that could sell for thousands.

Typically with coin collecting, the most valuable coins are either very old or contain mintage errors.

This is because there are usually fewer of them available, making them harder to find and more valuable to collectors.

If you're unsure how much a coin you have can sell for, a quick way to check its value is by searching the coin on eBay to see what it or similar coins have sold for in the past.

Other vendors and appraisers, like Coinappraiser and USA Coin Bank, can also help you learn about and sell your rare coins
