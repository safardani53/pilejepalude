Kendall Jenner, Bella Hadid and Rihanna reignite the 90s Adidas Popper Jogger trend but is

BUMBAGS, berets, small sunglasses and now 'popper joggers' have managed to transport themselves from the nineties to the present day.

While trousers that opened at the side with a simple (or sexy) rip were once just found in the wardrobes of male strippers, they were an everyday staple back in the nineties.

And now, twenty years later, they're all over the High Street... and celebs.

The impractical joggers have been spotted on Bella Hadid and Kendall Jenner, which means that it's only a matter of time before even your mum has a pair.

Kim Kardashian, 37, has even been seen in a pair, showing that you can jump on the trend even if you remember it first time round.

However, the joggers might look cool but they aren't the most practical slacks.

All it takes is to slightly catch one of the legs on a corner of a table for the whole thing to fall open and reveal your entire leg.

And while models might make walking around with them unpopped look effortlessly chic, in reality you will feel like you're caught having an argument with a cheap tent on a windy day.

People have been discussing the resurgence of the retro style on Twitter, with one girl lamenting: "nothing will make you feel fatter than putting on a pair of popper joggers and all the buttons simultaneously popping when you sit down."

Another added: "I know I'm not the most fashion conscious of folk. But please can popper joggers cease to exist?"
