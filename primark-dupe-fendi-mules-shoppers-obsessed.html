Primark launch 12 dupe of 550 Fendi mules &amp; shoppers are obsessed

THINK your feet deserve some designer clobber, but can't afford to foot the bill?

Primark is now selling a £12 version of designer mules and fashion fans are obsessed.

Although the designer version is emblazoned with Fendi's signature FF.
