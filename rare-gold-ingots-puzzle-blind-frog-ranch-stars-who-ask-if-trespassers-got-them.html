Rare gold ingots puzzle Blind Frog Ranch stars who ask if trespassers got them Sat

New Mystery at Blind Frog ranch episodes are arriving each week after season 3 kicked off last November. With an extraordinary amount of mysterious happenings on the Ollingers’ land, it’s no wonder the show is bringing more seasons to Discovery. Outside of the show, Duane and his co-star, Charlie Snider, are discussing even more Blind Frog Ranch mysteries, including “rare” gold ingots.

While attempting to search for gold he believes to be on his land, Duane Ollinger uncovers all kinds of happenings at his ranch that have thus far gone unexplained. Duane is joined by his son, Chad Ollinger, Charlie, and other experts in Mystery at Blind Frog Ranch season 3 in a bid to find a potentially vast fortune on his property.

Blind Frog Ranch mysterious gold ingots

Many mysterious things have been uncovered at Bling Frog Ranch over the past few years, and there are even more to investigate on the Discovery Channel show.

Taking to Instagram in December 2023, Charlie Snider shared a photograph of a gold ingot and wrote in the caption: “Where did 500 hundred of theses go to, you think the poachers and trespassers got them.”

Another post containing the same image was published on the same day, captioned: “What happened to 500 of these ingots. You think the poachers and Trespassers got them”

Many fans took to the comments section to give their opinion, with one writing: “No, my hunch is the poachers did not get lucky.”

Another wrote the Blind Frog Ranch gold ingots are rare: “They shouldn’t be hard to find! There’s not many out there like that! Loose lips sink ships!!!”

Duane also commented on the snap: “Maybe three different properties that they came from on the north end of BFR or south west of side of SL land or south east of BLM land or on BFR but not poachers land at all.”

Duane searches for gold at Blind Frog Ranch

Despite having a huge net worth, Duane is three seasons into hunting for gold on Blind Frog Ranch.

He and his team take fans along for the ride on the Discovery Channel show. Not only does he uncover hidden treasures on the ranch, but he also comes across phenomenons and liquid metal.

Outside of the series, Duane and Charlie often share some of their findings on the land including ‘bare’ footprints, mysterious art work, and ‘portals’ in the sky.
