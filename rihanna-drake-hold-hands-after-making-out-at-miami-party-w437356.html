Rihanna, Drake Hold Hands After 'Making Out' at Miami Party

Aubrih is making Us swoon! Rihanna and Drake were spotted walking hand in hand and acting flirty as they left an afterparty following his concert in Miami on Tuesday, August 30.

The rumored couple, who have yet to confirm their current relationship status, looked more official than ever as they emerged from E11EVEN nightclub holding hands and smiling.

Clad in a white shirt dress and stilettos, Rihanna, 28, showed off her long, toned legs as she carried a small purse and a bottle of Heineken beer. Drake, 29, rocked a black shirt and jeans before covering up in a denim jacket. The pair then cozied up in the car as they headed back to their hotel.

Inside the Miami hot spot, the duo “were making out and full-on kissing,” an onlooker tells Us Weekly.

Earlier in the night, the Barbadian beauty cheered on the “Controlla” rapper as he and Future brought their coheadlining Summer Sixteen tour to the American Airlines Arena.

Several fans inside captured videos of Rihanna in the audience, dancing and singing along to several of Drake’s songs, including his recent No. 1 single, “One Dance.”

As previously reported, the Toronto native recently professed his love for the “Love on the Brain” singer as he presented her with the Michael Jackson Video Vanguard Award at the 2016 MTV VMAs.

During his heartfelt speech on Sunday, August 28, Drake gushed, “She’s someone I’ve been in love with since I was 22 years old. She’s one of my best friends in the world.” Moments later, Rihanna gave him a hug and a quick kiss on the cheek before accepting the coveted prize.
