Charles Roger Pomfret Hodgson is an English musician, singer and songwriter, best known as a former co-head and founding member of the progressive rock band Supertramp. Hodgson composed and sang the majority of the band's hits such as "Dreamer", "Give a Little Bit", "Take the Long Way Home", "The Logical Song", "It's Raining Again" and "Breakfast in America". . Hodgson left Supertramp in 1983 and took the family off the Los Angeles music scene to live a simpler natural lifestyle and stay at home with the kids as they grow up. Hodgson returned to tour in 2001. He often writes on spiritual and philosophical topics, and his lyrics are described as personal and meaningful.
