Sea.Hear.Now 2022: How can I buy tickets?

SET IN Asbury Park, New Jersey, the Sea.Hear.Now festival will take place from September 17 until 18 of 2022.

Over 25 artists are set to perform across three stages.

How can I buy tickets?

Tickets are available via the Sea.Hear.Now official website.

The tickets come at various levels and prices, including:

  • Two-Day General Admission: $195, includes basic access to the festival
  • Two-Day General Admission Plus: $375, includes a private lounge and air-conditioned restrooms
  • Two-Day VIP: $685, includes exclusive viewing areas and VIP Lounge access
  • Two-Day Platinum: $1450, includes on-stage access, Platinum Lounge access, and complimentary food
  • Two-Day Ultimate Experience: $8750, includes a three-night stay at the Asbury Ocean Club Resort, unlimited Lounge access, backstage access, $200 food and drink credit, private golf cart transportation, and admission for two to an official Late Night Show of choice

Layaway plans are available for ticket purchases.

Who is performing at Sea.Hear.Now 2022?

On Saturday, September 17, 2022, the headlining performer is Stevie Nicks of Fleetwood Mac.

Also scheduled to play on Saturday is My Morning Jacket, Billy Strings, Gary Clark Jr, Fletcher, The Backseat Lovers, Peach Pit, Skip Marley, Celisse, Djo, Aly & AJ, The Surfrajettes, Dogs in a Pile, and Dentist.

The headliner for Sunday, September 18 is Green Day.

What is the Sea.Hear.Now festival?

First launching in 2018, Sea.Hear.Now is an annual music, art, and ocean sustainability festival which offers a professional surfing competition alongside various artist performances.

Held on the beachfront and boardwalk in Asbury Park, New Jersey; the festival offers a unique experience to concert-goers.
