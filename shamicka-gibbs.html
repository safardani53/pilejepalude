Shamicka Gibbs Biography

Shamicka Gibbs is an American citizen who came to the media limelight following her high-profile relationship. She first became the headline when her relationship with actor Martin Lawrence was revealed. The couple dated for 13 years before tying the knot in 2010. With his long list of films, he gained much recognition from the movie Bad Boys alongside Will Smith in 1995. He also appeared in the film series Big Momma's House and also worked as an executive producer. He won two Image Awards for Best Actor in a Comedy Series  Martin in 1995 and 1996. He also appeared in the multi-star film Wild Hogs and shared the screen with Tim Allen, John Travolta, and William H. Macy.
