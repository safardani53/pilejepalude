Soulja Boy Blasts Lil Durk, Migos And Others On IG live

During another of his Instagram Live sessions, Soulja Boy went berserk. He was shooting at Lil Durk, Migos, and others this time.

Soulja Boy probably spends more time on Instagram Live than he does in the studio these days, as he is seen dragging someone on the platform every other week.

In a recent Instagram Live, he discussed how he collaborated on songs with many rappers when they were relatively unknown, but they haven’t given him any credit or features since they rose to fame.

Soulja Blasts Lil Durk, Migos, And Others On IG Live

On March 30, 2022, the 31-year-old rapper took to Instagram and began airing his grievances.

“I always work with an artist before they pop off,” Soulja began before naming Migos, Rich The Kid, Famous Dex, Lil Durk, and other artists.

“Take a look at me and Migos. Take a look at Rich The Kid and me. Take a look at me and Mozzy. Take a look at me and Lil B. Take a look at me and Riff Raff. Famous Dex and I. “How many rapper n****s have I worked with before they got their record deal?”

“Take a look at me and Lil Durk. I was featured on Lil Durk’s debut mixtape. “Look at me and Chief Keef,” the rapper said before bringing up the Broadway Girls singer again.

“I write songs for all these n****s before they become famous.” Then, once they become famous, I can’t even get a song from them. That’s insane. But back when you all n****s were in the hood, and nobody knew who the f**k you all were, I was doing songs with you all n****s.”

Soulja Boy then went on to talk about how he assisted Lil Durk in his early days in the industry. “All a n**** has to do is Google Soulja Boy, Lil Durk, and “Eater.” This is this man’s first mixtape. Nobody had any idea who this man was.”

Soulja Boy’s facts are a little skewed in this case because he appeared on Durk’s third mixtape, Life Ain’t No Joke. I’m a Hitta, and I’m Still a Hitta were his first two commercial mixtapes, and both of them helped the rapper become well-known.

A Look At Lil Durk’s Eater Ft Soulja Boy

The remix was included on the rapper’s 2012 mixtape Life Ain’t No Joke.

Other songs on the mixtape included F**k Em (featuring French Montana), Days of Our Lives Lyrics, Animal Lyrics, and Homicide. “Riding around with one-hundred and fifties; they riding around saying we did it for the city,” Soulja Boy says in Eater. Riding around with a cocked hammer; Stacks On Deck, we’re on top; and me and Lil Durk aren’t going anywhere.” The full lyrics can be found on Genius. It should be noted, however, that explicit language has been used.

Fans Support Lil Durk After Soulja Boy’s Ranting

As soon as Soulja Boy began slamming Lil Durk, the majority of fans sided with him.

“If Lil Durk wasn’t on fire right now, Soulja Boy wouldn’t have mentioned his name, and I never heard a Soulja x Durk collab,” one fan wrote.

“You deserve a prize if you can recite a @souljaboy lyric that isn’t ten years old,” another fan said.
