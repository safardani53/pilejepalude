Olivia Flowers and Rod Razavi

Olivia began seeing season 9 newbie Rod in early 2023 after her breakup from Austen in late 2022. Olivia and Rod went on a few dates, which played out on season 9 of the Bravo series. However, following both the death of Olivia’s brother, Conner, in January 2023 and the drama surrounding Austen kissing Olivia’s BFF Taylor coming to light, Olivia and Rod’s romance fizzled out.

“We went through a lot on the show together. We went through a lot off the show. We spent a lot of time together not filming,” Rod exclusively told Us in November 2023. “And I think with the timing and everything that was going on, it just made sense to kind of pull back and make sure we could stay friends. So when I kind of sensed her pulling back emotionally, it was easier [to do the same].” He added that the exes are “good friends” despite pulling the plug on their romance.
