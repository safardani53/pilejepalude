Stacey Dash Celebrity Profile Hollywood Life

Bio:
Stacey Dash (born Stacey Lauretta Dash on Jan. 20, 1967 in The Bronx, New York City, New York) is an American actress and conservative news correspondent. She is best known for playing the part of Dionne, Cher’s best friend in the 1995 cult classic teen film Clueless alongside Alicia Silverstone. She starred in several other TV shows and films over the years, including Renaissance Man, Eve, CSI, and The Cosby Show. She also starred in several music videos, including Kanye West’s for “All Falls Down.” Later on, she gave up acting for political activism. She is a frequent contributor to Fox News Channel, and has been the origin of many controversial statements. She was suspended on Dec. 7 for saying “I felt like he could give a s*** — excuse me, like he could care less” about President Obama on-air. She appeared on the Feb. 28 Oscar telecast to wish everyone a “Happy Black History Month” after earlier saying of Fox News that it should be abolished.

Personal Life:  

Stacy is a mother of two children, a son and a daughter, and has been married and divorced three times to Brian Lovell, James Maby, and Emmanuel Xuereb. When she divorced Emmanuel in 2010, she had to file a restraining order against him for hiting “her on several occasions on her face, head and body throughout the marriage.” She switched from Democrat to Republican to support Mitt Romney in the 2012 election, which brought a lot of criticism from fans. In 2014 she started dating Michael Evers, a hot young guy 22 years her junior.
