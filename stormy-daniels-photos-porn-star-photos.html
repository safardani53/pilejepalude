Photos Of The Porn Star Who Allegedly Slept With Trump Hollywood Life

Stormy Daniels and her attorney Michael Avenatti leave federal court in New York in 2018. She wore a light pink blazer top over a black blouse and matching light pink skirt. 

Stormy, whose real name is Stephanie Clifford, is best known as a pornographic actress and former stripper. She won many industry awards and is a member of the NightMoves Hall of Fame, AVN Hall of Fame and XRCO Hall of Fame. She also stepped into the spotlight when she accused Donald Trump of giving her a $130,000 hush money payment before his 2016 presidential election campaign after an alleged affair between them.

Trump was arrested on Apr. 4, 2023 for the alleged hush money payment after it was considered a campaign violation. When asked about her reaction his indictment, Stormy said she had “mixed feelings’ but still wanted to see justice served.

‘A person in power is not exempt from the law,’ she told The Sunday Times. ‘And no matter what your job is, or what your bank account says, you’re held accountable for the things you’ve said and done, and justice is served. It’s bittersweet. He’s done so much worse that he should have been taken down [for] before. I am fully aware of the insanity of it, being a porn star.’
