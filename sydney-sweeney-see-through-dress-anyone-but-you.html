Busty Sydney Sweeney stuns in see-through white dress at premiere of new rom-com Anyone But You

SYDNEY Sweeney shows she’s sheer to stay with an eye-catching red carpet appearance.

The US actress, 26, flew to her namesake Aussie city for the premiere of rom-com Anyone But You.

And it was transparent to all that fans loved meeting her...and her see-through dress.

Breakout star Sydney has starred in a number of hit films and television serieseries over the past few years.

In 2018, she appeared as recurring character Eden in The Handmaid’s Tale.

Her character was dramatically killed off within a few episodes.

A year later, she snagged a role in Quentin Tarantino's 2019 dramedy, Once Upon a Time in Hollywood.

She went on to play Cassie Howard in the cult HBO drama Euphoria.

In 2021, she had a leading role in another HBO series, black comedy The White Lotus.

Sydney played Olivia, a young student who makes up a group of holidaymakers enjoying themselves in Hawaii.
