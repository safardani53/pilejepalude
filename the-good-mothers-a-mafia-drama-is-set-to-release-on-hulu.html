The Good Mothers A Mafia Drama Is Set To Release On Hulu

According to CinemaBlend, The Good Mothers, a new Italian crime thriller, will debut in the United States on Hulu on Wednesday, April 5, 2023, at 12:01 a.m. ET, and worldwide on Disney+ on the same day at 3:01 a.m. ET. The series is based on the same-named novel by renowned author Alex Perry. It recounts the tale of three women who went to work for an infamous Italian crime group to assist a prosecution in bringing down the kingdom. Julian Jarrold and Elisa Amoruso direct the show.

The Good Mothers video depicts three brave women’s attempts to bring down a crime organization.

On March 27, 2023, Hulu released the official video for The Good Mothers, providing a glimpse into the numerous exciting events set to occur in the new show.  The video begins with a terrifying scenario of a lady fleeing, crying for her mother, and being apprehended by her father. The video then depicts a few important scenes from the program, but there are no significant reveals. Overall, it retains an eerie tone, concentrating primarily on ambiance while not sacrificing character growth. Along with the teaser, Hulu released a synopsis of the program, which reads:

”The plot revolves around Anna Colace, an intelligent, youthful lawyer who collaborates with the women of the ‘Ndrangheta to bring the infamous crime group down from within. The domineering male system of the criminal organization brutally subjugated and ignored the women, moms, and wives of the top leaders.”

The description continues,

”Anna’s experience begins with the infamous abduction of Lea Garofalo, who promised to testify against her husband, Carlo Cosco, in order to flee his dominance and help her daughter, Denise, grow up in a less poisonous environment.”

Finally, the summary says,

”Anna’s investigation delves deeper into the murky world of the ‘Ndrangheta and the powerful stories of Giuseppina Pesce and Concetta Cacciola – two very different women who share a dramatic background; an oppressive and suffocating existence, and the desire and drive to escape in order to give themselves and their children a better future, just as Lea did.”

Viewers can expect a strong character-driven tale of courage, resolve, and virtue as the three women set out to bring down an infamous crime dynasty based on the teaser and synopsis. The program is said to have six segments, all of which are set to premiere on Hulu and Disney+ on April 5, 2023.

Who is the cast of The Good Mothers? Details about the cast

Barbara Chichiarelli plays Anna Colace, a youthful and skilled lawyer in The Good Mothers. Aside from the three women, she is one of the film’s main characters, and fans can anticipate Barbara to give an unforgettable performance on the program. Barabara Chichiarelli has appeared in a number of films, including Bad Tales, Suburra: Blood on Rome, and La Compagnia del Cigno.

Valentina Bellè as Giuseppina Pesce, Gaia Girace as Denise Cosco, Francesco Colella as Carlo Cosco, and many others round out the ensemble. The Good Mothers will be available on Hulu and Disney+ on Wednesday, April 5, 2023.
