'The Hills' Original Cast: Where Are They Now?

When The Hills first debuted in May 2006, it looked a lot different than the revival, The Hills: New Beginnings. No matter what version they’re watching, fans are still hooked on the cast’s unwritten chapters.

The MTV reality show, a spinoff of Laguna Beach, followed Lauren Conrad from Laguna to Los Angeles, where she moved in with Heidi Montag. Their “first friend,” who also lived in the building — and later lived with Conrad — was Audrina Patridge.

While attending the Fashion Institute of Design & Merchandising, Conrad quickly landed an internship at Teen Vogue and met fellow intern Whitney Port. Montag, meanwhile, left school shortly after the season began when she landed a job at Bolthouse Productions.

Throughout the series, viewers watched as Conrad’s former flame Jason Wahler made his way back into her life and saw Montag’s love story with Spencer Pratt from its beginning.
