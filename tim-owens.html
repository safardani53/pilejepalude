Who is Tim Owens:

Tim Owens is a famous Metal Singer. He was born on September 13, 1967 and his birthplace is Akron, OH. Tim is also well known as, Heavy metal singer and shredder who sang for Judas Priest and Iced Earth and then moved on to Charred Walls of the Damned. 

Tim is originated from United States. He replaced Rob Halford as the lead singer of Judas Priest, continuing his predecessor’s trademark high-pitched screams.
