TJ Holmes has allegedly cheated on his wife with several women at ABC before his affair with Amy Robach.

The Good Morning America anchor and his co-host were outed last week and have since both been taken off air as the allegations about TJ continue to roll in.

According to Page Six, TJ, 45, had an affair with at least one other woman in addition to Amy, 49, and GMA producer Natasha Singh, 37.

Multiple sources told the outlet that he had a relationship with another staffer while working at the network and while married to his wife, Marilee Fiebig.

Both TJ and Amy reportedly left their spouses in August.

Their affair is believed to have been going on since June, although some sources have claimed that both had split from their respective partners by the time the rendezvous began.

A source told The U.S. Sun: "They were taken off the air to calm things down."

"There has been no determination of any violation of any ABC policies by either one of them," a second insider added.
