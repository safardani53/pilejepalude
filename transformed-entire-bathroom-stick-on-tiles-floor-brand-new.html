A PAIR of DIY pros have revealed how she gave her bathroom a budget makeover using only stick-on tiles - and people can’t believe how professional it looks. 

Danielle and Nathan were sick of their bog-standard shower room, decorated in a range of white and grey tiles. 

So they decided to give it an upgrade using cheap stick-on tiles from Shein - and were stunned at how good the finished result looked. 

Danielle said: She said: “I’m using these stick-on tiles in the whole house.” 

During the 13-second video, Danielle reveals what their bathroom looked like before, almost entirely white apart from the grey square tiles that covered the floor. 

Their white porcelain sink and bath are surrounded by white square tiles, only broken up by black grout in between. 

MORE TOP DIY TIPS

To transform the space, Danielle started by covering the floor with stick-on tiles, decorated with a white, pink, and orange mosaic design. 

For a seamless look, she used a retractable knife to cut them to size around her bath and sink. 

Danielle then went on to add vertical strips of pink vinyl tape to her shower screen to give it a quick glow-up. 

She also covered the tiles in her shower with a vinyl sheet decorated with cartoon plants and cacti, before adding a pink bath board to finish off the look.
