Elvis Presley impersonator shares shocking moment 'granny gropes his privates on stage in the middle

A DAD of eight who moonlights as a professional Elvis impersonator has hit out at a granny for 'groping' him on stage - and fumed that the real King of Rock and Roll wouldn't put up with it.

Shocking footage shows the moment Lee Grindey, 41, was groped by the reveller while working at a festival three weeks ago.

It shows the woman - thought to be a grandmother in her 50s - appearing to back into Lee and grab his genitals before dancing off with a pal.

Lee shared the video on Facebook to raise awareness of sexual assault - but the woman's family slammed HIM for making the footage public.

He blasted: "Elvis would never have accepted that, so I won't.

"I'm hoping to raise awareness and show not only women are victims of such assault.

"It isn't a bit of fun. If it was the other way round I'd be going through the courts by now.

"The woman who did it thought it was great. For me, there was just shock."

Lee, from Stoke-in-Trent, waived his right to anonymity as the victim of sexual assault to emphasise that groping performers isn't acceptable.

He added: "As times gone on and I've mulled over it, I've had messages from members of her family telling I'm in the wrong for publishing it on Facebook.

"They were telling me I'd done something wrong. I've had one message and Katrina's had a couple.

"I've had someone describe her as 'mum' contacted me. She's definitely a mum, possibly a grandma.
