How to vote in Dancing On Ice 2019 - voting, cost and app details explained

DANCING on Ice is back on our screens with the final is tonight and soon the winner will be crowned.

But how can you vote for your favourite? Here are all the details you need.

How can I vote on Dancing On Ice?

By mobile

You can vote using your mobile phone as ITV uses short dial codes (MSDCs) which means there is no added Network Access Charge.

All votes by mobile cost a fixed fee of 35p.

To vote via mobile dial 650 51 + the two digits corresponding to your chosen celebrity.

You can also vote from your mobile using the 09 number but there's likely to be an added network charge.

It is no longer possible to vote by text.

Dancing on Ice News

Online

You can also vote online, by going to itv.com/vote where Dancing on Ice 2022 will be the only option which appears.

You have five votes and can access the website both online and by scanning the in-show QR code.

When is Dancing on Ice on TV?

Dancing on Ice returned to ITV in 2022 and airs on the channel on March 27 from 7pm.

It is presented by Holly Willoughby and Phillip Schofield.

Skating icons and current judges Jayne Torvill and Christopher Dean are back as judges, alongside Ashley Banjo and Oti Mabuse.
