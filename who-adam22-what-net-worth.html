ADAM22 is a content creator and podcast host.

From his online career to his family life - here's everything you need to know.

Who is Adam22?

Adam Grandmaison was born on November 24, 1983, in Nashua, New Hampshire, USA.

He is a podcaster and YouTuber, more commonly known online as Adam22.

In addition to podcasting, Adam22 is also a rapper, record executive, and BMX rider.

He also has an OnlyFans account alongside his wife Lena Nersesian.

Aside from being a popular podcast host, Adam22 created his own BMX website called The Come Up which features videos from his BMX team.

Additionally, he also created a clothing line in support of his BMX team.

In 2017, the online creator hosted the Trap Circus Music Festival in Miami, Florida.

As of July 2023, Adam22 has amassed 1.5 million followers on Instagram.

What podcast does Adam22 host?

Adam22 is best known for being the creator and host of the podcast No Jumper - where he talks with public figures who discuss their journey to success.

Initially, the podcast which launched in 2015, attracted guests such as the late Juice WRLD, Tekashi69, and the late XXXTentacion.

However, as the podcast developed, Adam22 went on to also interview actors, music critics and producers.

As of July 2023, Adam22's YouTube channel dedicated to his podcast has 4.62 million subscribers.

Who is Adam22's wife Lena the Plug?

Adam22 is married to YouTuber and adult star Lena Nersesian, or more commonly known online as Lena the Plug.

The pair married in May 2023, following a five-year relationship.

She was born on June 1, 1991, in California.

Lena began as a model on social media platforms Snapchat and Instagram, before transitioning to YouTube in 2016.

Her first videos were mainly daily vlogs and Q&As.

Lena then went on to become an adult star amid calls for ''premium'' content.

In January 2017, she faced a brutal backlash online after promising to release a sex tape if she reached one million subscribers.

That same year Lena stunned viewers once more when she revealed the reason she let her best friend sleep with her then boyfriend, Adam.

Like her husband, Lena has also ventured into the podcasting world.

Lena and Adam22 have both launched a podcast called Plug Talk, which focuses on interviewing adult film stars on OnlyFans.

It premiered on November 16, 2021, with guest Adriana Chechik.

In 2022, Lena made the decision to launch a solo podcast, titled Touchy Subject, however, it only lasted a few months.

As of July 2023, Lena has 1.2 million followers on Instagram and 1.6 million subscribers on YouTube.
