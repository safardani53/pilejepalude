Rachel Crow Biography

Rachel Crow was born on a Friday, January 23, 1998 in Mead, CO. Her birth name is Rachel Kelly Crow and she is currently 25 years old. People born on January 23 fall under the zodiac sign of Aquarius. Her zodiac animal is Tiger.

Rachel Kelly Crow is an American singer, comedienne, and actress from Mead, Colorado. Crow participated in the first season of the U.S. version of The X Factor in 2011 and she finished in 5th place overall. After being eliminated from The X Factor, Crow met with Disney for possible roles in future Disney productions. She’s appeared on many TV shows, including BrainSurge, Inside Edition, Big Time Rush, Figure It Out and The Wendy Williams Show. She also had a role in Fred: The Show as Starr. Her debut EP, Rachel Crow, debuted on iTunes charts on number 71. She voiced Carla in her first film role, Rio 2, and she sang some lines in the film from the song ‘Batucada Familia’ (2014).

She made her first stage performance at the age of six and sang her first song when she was eighteen months years old.
