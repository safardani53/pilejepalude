Winter Wonderland stabbing - Man, 24, knifed 'in the neck' at Hyde Park as cops rush to scene

HORROR descended outside Hyde Park's Winter Wonderland as a 24-year-old man was stabbed 'in the neck.'

The Met Police has confirmed officers were called to an area just outside the London festive attraction just before 9.30pm on Sunday December 11.

An eyewitness described the attack in Hyde Park and reported someone was "stabbed in the neck."

The victim, a 24-year-old man, was rushed to hospital by emergency services.

A spokesperson for the Met Police said: "The victim is in hospital with a slash wound, it is not life-threatening or life-changing.

"No arrests have been made."

A TikTok posted by a witness at the scene showed police cordoning off an area just outside Winter Wonderland.

A Hyde Park Winter Wonderland spokesperson said: “We are aware of an incident last night involving an individual travelling away from Hyde Park Winter Wonderland.

"We are working with the relevant authorities to understand more and support their investigations.”

Police also called for any witnesses who are yet to come forward to call them on 101 and reference crime number 6280/11dec.
