Quinn Pensky and Logan Reese relationship timeline: When did the Zoey 102 characters get together?

ONE of the most shocking moments in late 2000s television was when Quinn Pensky and Logan Reese got together on Zoey 101.

Logan, known for his jock attitude and sleeveless shirts, somehow managed to woo brainiac Quinn, and now, they're getting married in the upcoming Zoey 101 sequel, Zoey 102.

When did Quinn Pensky and Logan Reese start dating?

Zoey 101 was a popular television series in the 2000s that followed the lives of Zoey Brooks (Jamie Lynn Spears) and her friends as they attended boarding school at Pacific Coast Academy (PCA) in Malibu, California.

For three seasons, fans watched as two of the main characters, Logan Reese (Matthew Underwood) and Quinn Pensky (Erin Sanders) butted heads at every turn.

Several times throughout the series, Logan and his roommates, Chase (Sean Flynn) and Michael (Christopher Massey) made fun of Quinn and her then-boyfriend, Mark Del Figgalo (Jack Salvatore Jr.), which is why viewers were shocked when they got together during Season 3.

Their relationship started in the episode Quinn Misses the Mark, which originally aired on February 24, 2008.

During the episode, Mark breaks up with Quinn, and after she sees him with other girls, she decides to give herself a makeover.

Zoey tried to reason with Quinn and assure her that she doesn't need to change, but she wouldn't listen.

"Look, if looking like this is what gets Mark's attention, then here I am," Quinn told Zoey.

Later on in the episode, Quinn approaches Mark, but he wasn't as thrilled with her new look as she thought he would be.

"What are you doing?" Mark asked Quinn.

"What do you mean?" she responded.

"Your face, your hair, your lack of glasses, your," he started to say, before Quinn cut him off, asking "Ya like?"

"No, this isn't you," he said.

Mark went on to explain that he's dating his new girlfriend, Brooke because they "really connected" over their similar interests.

"I'd rather not hear about your magical connections with Brooke," Quinn quipped.

"I'm just saying, I like her for her," Mark continued. "Her hotness is just gravy."

Mark's response made Quinn upset so she stormed away and went to go cry on a bench, which is when Logan found her.

"What's your problem?" he asked Quinn.

"Nothing Logan, just keep riding," she responded.

Despite Quinn's hostility towards him, he sat down next to her and asked what was wrong, which showed off a softer side of the character that fans had yet to see.

She then proceeded to confide in Logan and explain how she was upset about Mark dumping her.

"Well if it makes you feel any better, I think Mark's an idiot...He's an idiot for breaking up with you," Logan said.

"...Look, we all know you're weird, but you're smart, you're pretty and you're kind of fun."

Logan then asked why Quinn was dressed the way she was, and when she said that it was to "compete with Brooke" he said that she shouldn't.

He then proceeded to take her glasses out of her bag and put them on her face.

"Hey, there's Quinn," he says before kissing her.

Afterward, they started sneaking around trying to hide their relationship from their friends because they were embarrassed to be seen together publicly.

They would pick fake fights with one another in an attempt to cover it up, but in the end, love won.

Logan finally revealed their relationship to the entire school while at the prom after Stacey Dillsen (Abby Wilde) tried to kiss him.

"Why wouldn't you want to kiss me? My lips are moist," Stacey asked, to which Logan responded, "Because I love Quinn!"

"That's right, I love Quinn Penski!" he declared.

"And I love Logan Reese," Quinn declared back.

How can I watch Zoey 102?

Quinn and Logan's relationship was one of the biggest plot twists in Zoey 101 history, and now, it will be the subject of Paramount+'s newest film, Zoey 102.

"Now in her twenties, Zoey Brooks is still trying to figure out life and love. When Quinn and Logan announce they’re getting married, the PCA gang returns to Malibu for an over-the-top wedding that turns into a high school reunion for the books," the film's description reads.

Zoey 102 will bring back several beloved characters, including Zoey, Chase, Quinn, Michael, Logan, Stacey, and Mark, as well as introduce fans to new ones.

The one-hour and 41 minute movie can be streamed exclusively on Paramount+ starting July 27, 2023.
